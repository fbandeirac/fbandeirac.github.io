About me

How I Think About Engineering

Backend systems are only as good as the thinking behind them. I spend my career asking the right questions before writing code: How does this scale? What happens when it fails? Who maintains this in two years? Over 8+ years in production systems, I've learned that resilience isn't a feature you add at the end—it's a design principle from day one.

I've migrated 10K+ users from legacy systems to cloud-native stacks. I've led teams through chaos. I've built CI/CD pipelines that let people ship with confidence. And I've learned that the best technical decisions come from understanding both the system and the people who operate it.

Right now, I'm excited about AI-augmented workflows—not as a replacement for thinking, but as a tool that lets engineers spend less time on pattern-matching and more time on design. I also care deeply about mentorship and building teams that enjoy the work and each other.
